My Creative Process

All of my Web development and design projects are based off of a practiced formula to get the result that I am looking for

1

Discuss The Project

The first step of any project is to have a conversation with the client. I like to learn about their goals, vision, and expectations for the project. This helps me to understand what they're looking for and how I can best meet their needs. I like to get to know my clients and build a relationship with them so we can work together effectively.

2

Brainstorming ^ Concept

During the brainstorming stage, I focus on generating new ideas that are creative, fresh, and unexpected. This is a time for experimentation and out-of-the-box thinking. While not every idea will be a winner, the process of brainstorming allows me to discover new and exciting possibilities.

3

Web dev Planning

In the web dev planning stage, I work with the client to create a blueprint for the project. This includes determining the features and functionality of the website, as well as deciding on a content management system (CMS) and other technical details. This stage is crucial in ensuring that the website is well-designed and user-friedly.

4

Development and Implementation

Once the design and planning stages are complete, I move on to actually building the website. This involves writing code, testing it, and then deploying it to a server so it can be accessed by users. I also make sure to optimize the website for speed and security, and to follow best practices for SEO and accessibility.
